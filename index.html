Patrick Star

Patrick Star is SpongeBob's best friend. He is a pink, overweight sea star who lives under a rock. Patrick is known for his low intelligence, laziness, and love for jellyfishing with SpongeBob. Despite his dimwitted nature, Patrick is good-hearted and always means well.

SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is an optimistic and energetic sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, where he is exceptionally skilled at making Krabby Patties. SpongeBob is known for his square shape, bright yellow color, and enthusiastic personality. He loves going on adventures and trying new things, often with his best friend, Patrick Star.

Squidward J.Q. Tentacles

Squidward Tentacles is SpongeBob's neighbor and coworker at the Krusty Krab. He is a turquoise-colored octopus with a pretentious attitude and a love for art and music. Squidward is often annoyed by SpongeBob and Patrick's antics and dreams of a peaceful and sophisticated life away from them.

Sandy Cheeks

Sandy Cheeks is a squirrel from Texas who lives in an air dome under the sea. She is an inventor and scientist known for her Texas traits, including her strong accent and rodeo skills. Sandy is adventurous and loves practicing karate with SpongeBob. She wears a diving suit to breathe underwater.

Eugene Harold Krabs

Eugene H. Krabs is the greedy owner of the Krusty Krab and SpongeBob's boss. He is a red crab who is obsessed with money and will go to great lengths to make a profit. Mr. Krabs is a single father to his teenage daughter, Pearl. Despite his greed, Mr. Krabs cares deeply for his employees and his restaurant.

Sheldon J. Plankton

Sheldon J. Plankton is a small, green planktonic copepod and the main antagonist of the series. He runs the Chum Bucket, a failing restaurant across the street from the Krusty Krab. Plankton's life goal is to steal the Krabby Patty formula from Mr. Krabs. He is constantly devising plans to achieve this, though they invariably fail.
